Riverock - Thirst for better

About:

This was a programme held within ITT for a selected group of 3rd year students. Our job as to work in teams to create an out of home ad campaign for Deep River Rock water. I acted as account manager and creative manager for the team.
My responsibilities included:

  • Reporting to our coordinator on a regular basis with our progress,
  • Managing my team to focus on current tasks, and motivating them through the times of high pressure,
  • Creating an effective creative that reflected the results of primary and secondary market research.
From market research, the big idea to media spend, myself and my team created a campaign we felt represented the brand, and their sponsorships well. It was an enjoyable and busy experience to learn exactly ow to carry out a full out of home campaign.
